Expert Agricultural Financing

Janus AG Finance specializes in real estate loans for the agricultural community at competitive rates. With over 30 years of experience in agricultural lending, we understand the unique needs of farmers and ranchers.

Why Choose Janus for Your Farm and Ranch Loan?

  • Competitive Rates: We offer some of the most competitive interest rates in the agricultural lending market.
  • Flexible Terms: Customized loan terms that work with your farming operation's cash flow.
  • Quick Approval: Our streamlined process gets you approved faster than traditional lenders.
  • Agricultural Expertise: Our team understands farming operations, seasonal cash flows, and agricultural real estate.
  • Personalized Service: Work directly with experienced agricultural loan specialists.

What We Finance

  • Farm and ranch land purchases
  • Agricultural property refinancing
  • Farm expansion and development
  • Equipment and infrastructure improvements
  • Working capital for operations

Loan Features

  • Loan amounts from $100,000 to $10+ million
  • Fixed and adjustable rate options
  • Terms up to 30 years
  • Low down payment options available
  • No prepayment penalties

Application Process

Getting started is easy. Simply fill out our pre-qualification form or contact us directly to speak with one of our agricultural lending specialists. We'll review your application and get back to you within 24-48 hours.
