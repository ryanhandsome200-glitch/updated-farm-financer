Specialized Vineyard Financing

We offer a variety of loan products and the expertise to create a financing package that will meet your vineyard's specific goals. Whether you're establishing a new vineyard, expanding operations, or refinancing existing debt, we understand the unique needs of wine grape production.

Vineyard Operations We Finance

  • Established Vineyards: Mature vineyards with proven production
  • New Vineyard Development: Land preparation and vine planting
  • Vineyard Expansion: Adding acreage to existing operations
  • Winery Operations: Combined vineyard and winery facilities
  • Vineyard Improvements: Irrigation, trellising, and infrastructure

What We Finance

  • Vineyard land purchases
  • Vineyard development and establishment
  • Property refinancing
  • Infrastructure improvements
  • Equipment and machinery
  • Working capital
