Bridge Loans

Short-term financing for immediate needs

Short-Term Agricultural Bridge Financing

The purpose of a bridge loan is to fund an immediate need to improve, expand, or transition with the intent of refinancing into a lower interest loan in 1 to 3 years. Bridge loans provide quick access to capital when timing is critical.

When to Use a Bridge Loan

  • Property Improvements: Fund improvements that will increase property value before refinancing
  • Expansion Projects: Finance expansion while waiting for permanent financing
  • Property Transitions: Bridge the gap during property acquisition or sale
  • Time-Sensitive Opportunities: Act quickly on time-sensitive opportunities
  • Seasonal Cash Flow: Manage seasonal cash flow gaps

How Bridge Loans Work

Bridge loans are short-term financing solutions typically lasting 1-3 years. They're designed to provide immediate capital with the understanding that you'll refinance into a permanent loan once improvements are complete or conditions improve.

Benefits

  • Quick approval and funding
  • Flexible terms
  • Interest-only payment options
  • No prepayment penalties
  • Designed for refinancing

Typical Terms

  • Loan term: 1-3 years
  • Interest-only payments available
  • Competitive rates for short-term financing
  • Flexible collateral requirements
  • Streamlined application process
