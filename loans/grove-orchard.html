Specialized Tree Crop Financing

Janus Ag Finance provides loans for citrus, almond, avocado, walnut, and other grove and orchard operations. We understand the unique requirements of tree crop farming, including long-term investment horizons and seasonal production cycles.

Crops We Finance

  • Citrus: Oranges, lemons, grapefruits, and other citrus varieties
  • Nuts: Almonds, walnuts, pistachios, and other nut trees
  • Avocados: Commercial avocado groves
  • Stone Fruits: Peaches, plums, cherries, and other stone fruits
  • Other Tree Crops: Apples, pears, and other orchard operations

What We Finance

  • Grove and orchard land purchases
  • Property refinancing
  • Orchard expansion and development
  • Irrigation system improvements
  • Equipment and infrastructure
  • Working capital for operations

Why Choose Us

  • Understanding of tree crop production cycles
  • Knowledge of crop-specific valuation
  • Flexible terms that account for establishment periods
  • Competitive rates for agricultural operations
  • Expert underwriting for specialty crops

Loan Features

  • Loan amounts tailored to your operation size
  • Flexible repayment terms
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Understanding of seasonal cash flows
  • Long-term financing options
