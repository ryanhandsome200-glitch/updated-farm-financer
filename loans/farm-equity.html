Farm Equity Line-of-Credit

Access funds when you need them at competitive rates

Flexible Financing for Your Farm

Having money available when you need it at a great rate is possible with our Line-of-Credit. This flexible financing solution allows you to access funds as needed, making it perfect for managing cash flow, seasonal expenses, and unexpected opportunities.

How It Works

A farm equity line of credit uses the equity in your agricultural property as collateral. You're approved for a maximum credit limit, and you can draw funds as needed. You only pay interest on the amount you use, not the full credit limit.

Benefits

  • Flexible Access: Draw funds when you need them, up to your credit limit
  • Pay Interest Only: Only pay interest on the amount you use
  • Competitive Rates: Lower interest rates than credit cards or unsecured loans
  • Revolving Credit: As you pay down the balance, funds become available again
  • Tax Benefits: Interest may be tax-deductible for business use

Common Uses

  • Seasonal operating expenses
  • Equipment purchases
  • Property improvements
  • Working capital needs
  • Emergency expenses
  • Opportunity purchases

Features

  • Credit limits based on property equity
  • Competitive variable interest rates
  • Interest-only payment options available
  • No prepayment penalties
  • Easy access via check or online transfer
