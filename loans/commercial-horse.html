Commercial Horse Properties

Financing for boarding, training, and breeding operations

Specialized Horse Property Financing

We are proud to offer financing to boarding, training and horse breeding operations generating at least $10,000 in yearly revenue. Our team understands the unique requirements of commercial horse properties and can structure loans that work with your business model.

Eligible Operations

  • Horse Boarding Facilities: Full-service boarding operations with stables, paddocks, and amenities
  • Training Facilities: Professional training operations for performance horses
  • Breeding Operations: Commercial breeding facilities with proven revenue streams
  • Equestrian Centers: Multi-purpose facilities offering lessons, clinics, and events
  • Riding Schools: Educational facilities with consistent revenue

Minimum Requirements

  • Minimum $10,000 annual revenue
  • Established business operations (typically 2+ years)
  • Property suitable for commercial horse operations
  • Proper zoning and permits

What We Finance

  • Property purchase for commercial horse operations
  • Facility expansion and improvements
  • Refinancing existing horse property loans
  • Construction of new facilities
  • Equipment and infrastructure upgrades

Loan Benefits

  • Competitive interest rates
  • Flexible repayment terms
  • Understanding of seasonal revenue fluctuations
  • Quick approval process
  • Expert knowledge of horse property valuation
