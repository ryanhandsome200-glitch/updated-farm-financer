Solar Power Financing for Farms

Renewable energy financing for agricultural operations

Solar Energy Solutions for Agriculture

Solar power financing helps agricultural property owners install solar panels to generate renewable energy. This investment can significantly reduce energy costs, provide additional revenue through net metering, and contribute to sustainable farming practices.

What We Finance

  • Solar Panel Installation: Commercial and residential solar systems
  • Ground-Mounted Systems: Large-scale solar arrays on agricultural land
  • Rooftop Systems: Solar panels on farm buildings and structures
  • Battery Storage: Energy storage systems for reliability
  • Infrastructure: Mounting systems, inverters, and electrical work

Benefits

  • Significant reduction in energy costs
  • Additional revenue from excess energy production
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Potential tax credits and incentives
  • Energy independence and reliability
  • Increased property value

Project Types

  • Farm operation solar systems
  • Agrivoltaics (solar with agriculture)
  • Community solar projects
  • Large-scale solar farms

Loan Features

  • Competitive financing rates
  • Flexible repayment terms
  • Understanding of solar project economics
  • Expert guidance on system design
  • Long-term financing options
