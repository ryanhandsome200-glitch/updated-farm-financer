Broker Partnership Program

Janus AG Finance works with mortgage brokers and real estate professionals to provide agricultural financing solutions for their clients. Our broker program offers competitive rates, quick turnaround, and expert support for agricultural property loans.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Agricultural Expertise: Deep knowledge of farm and ranch financing
  • Competitive Rates: Attractive rates for your clients
  • Quick Processing: Fast approval and closing times
  • Broker Support: Dedicated support for broker partners
  • Wide Range of Products: Multiple loan types for various needs
  • Competitive Compensation: Attractive commission structure

Loan Products Available

  • Farm and Ranch Loans
  • Commercial Horse Properties
  • Rural Residential Loans
  • Grove and Orchard Loans
  • Vineyard Loans
  • Bridge Loans
  • Farm Equity Lines of Credit
  • And more specialized agricultural loans

How It Works

  1. Become a Partner: Contact us to join our broker network
  2. Submit Applications: Refer clients and submit loan applications
  3. We Process: Our team handles underwriting and processing
  4. Close Loans: We coordinate closing and you earn commission

Broker Benefits

  • Access to specialized agricultural loan products
  • Expert underwriting for agricultural properties
  • Competitive compensation
  • Marketing support and materials
  • Training on agricultural lending
  • Dedicated broker support team
