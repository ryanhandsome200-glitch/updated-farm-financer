Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

Common Questions

What types of properties do you finance?

We finance a wide variety of agricultural properties including farms, ranches, commercial horse properties, groves, orchards, vineyards, and rural residential properties.

What are your minimum loan amounts?

Loan minimums vary by loan type, but we typically work with loans starting at $100,000. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How long does the approval process take?

Our streamlined process typically provides initial feedback within 24-48 hours. Full approval depends on property evaluation and documentation, but we work to expedite the process.

What interest rates do you offer?

Interest rates vary based on loan type, property, and borrower qualifications. We offer competitive rates for agricultural loans. Contact us for current rate information.

Do you require a down payment?

Down payment requirements vary by loan type and property. We offer flexible options and can discuss what works best for your situation.

What states do you serve?

We serve agricultural properties across multiple states. See our States We Serve page for more information.

Can I refinance an existing agricultural loan?

Yes, we offer refinancing options for existing agricultural loans. This can help you secure better rates or terms.

Do you finance renewable energy projects?

Yes, we offer financing for solar and wind power projects on agricultural properties.

What documentation will I need?

Documentation requirements vary by loan type, but typically include property information, financial statements, tax returns, and business information for commercial operations.

How do I get started?

You can contact us by phone at 303-847-0611, email at bob@janusmortgage.com, or fill out our pre-qualification form online.
