Lending Process

Simple, streamlined, and efficient

How Our Lending Process Works

Our lending process is designed to be straightforward and efficient while ensuring we understand your needs and provide the best financing solution.

Step 1: Initial Consultation

Contact us by phone, email, or fill out our pre-qualification form. We'll discuss your financing needs and answer any questions you have.

Step 2: Application

Complete our loan application with basic information about your property and financing needs. Our team will guide you through the process.

Step 3: Documentation

We'll request necessary documentation including property information, financial statements, and other relevant materials. Our team will help you gather everything needed.

Step 4: Property Evaluation

We partner with local appraisers who understand agricultural properties to ensure accurate valuation of your property.

Step 5: Underwriting

Our experienced underwriters review your application with expertise in agricultural lending. We understand the unique aspects of farm and ranch operations.

Step 6: Approval & Terms

Once approved, we'll present you with loan terms and answer any questions. We work to create terms that fit your operation.

Step 7: Closing

We coordinate the closing process to make it as smooth as possible. Our team handles the details so you can focus on your operation.

Step 8: Ongoing Relationship

After closing, we maintain our relationship and are available to assist with future financing needs or questions.

What You'll Need

  • Property information and documentation
  • Financial statements (if applicable)
  • Tax returns
  • Business information (for commercial operations)
  • Any existing loan documentation
